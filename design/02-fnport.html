nesting Function-valued ports nest cleanly: a higher-order function inside an inline subgraph gets its own fn-port, which can itself open another subgraph. The breadcrumb in the corner of the subgraph zone (depth N) reflects how deep you are. There's no hard limit, but anything past depth 2 is usually a sign to promote-to-named.
